App inventor Project

"Video Wall"

This Project was made in CSP 1st Period

We were given with multiple app choices and we ened up with our 3rd pick: The Video Wall. We started working on it and we soon found out that the App Inventor had multiple flaws. In the end we found out ways to solve our issues. Two valuable skills I leared are problem solving skills and improvising.
