Robotics & Engineering Project

Factory Cell

This Project was made in Robotics & Engineering class in 5th Period

This was a project that I worked on for my Robotics & Engineering class when I was in Middle School. For this project, I was put in a team and we were supposed to assemble a component for a factory. The factory would consist of multiple components that the other teams around the class would make. Right off the bat, my team and I made a plan. We agreed that I would make the design and help with some of the building while the rest of the team members would build and program. Two skills I have learned in this project is Planning and Collaberation.
